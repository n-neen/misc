r-jump steps:

1. align with ledge
2. force standup in direction you will be r-jumping
3. force crouch
4. continue to hold down
5. hold down + R
6. pause
7. near the end of fadeout, release R, press jump as late as possible
8. unpause
9. when screen is fully black, hold jump + down-[direction] (for sb in vanilla and cliffhanger early varia, this is down-right)
